Yu djum information

Gudeg Yu Djum is the main kitchen and traditional food stall in Yogyakarta, started by a woman named Yu Djum, named Djuwariah. For a woman who is the "front shop" of the Djuwariah family, this is a way of living. Yu Djum's story is the story of a family. Djuwariah first sold his gudeg in Wijilan village, precisely to the south of Keraton, Yogyakarta. Every day he sells his gudeg by using a simple pole to a horse in the morning, little by little he accumulated capital to buy land and build a house. In 1950, the Gudeg Yu Djum food stall opened in Wijilan.
