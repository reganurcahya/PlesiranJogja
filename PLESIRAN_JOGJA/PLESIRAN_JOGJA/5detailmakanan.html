About gudeg

Yogyakarta, known as a city of culture and education, also has a strong reputation in terms of culinary delights. One of the culinary icons that you must try when visiting this city is gudeg. This dish is made of young jackfruit which is then boiled along with coconut and traditional herbs. Gudeg has a sweet taste and is usually served with krecek (spicy buffalo skin), chicken, eggs, tofu, tempeh, and white rice. The taste of gudeg is a perfect combination of sweet and savory from the side dishes. Visiting Yogyakarta without tasting gudeg is certainly a big miss. With a combination of rich flavors, deep cultural values, and a unique culinary experience, gudeg is one dish that every tourist must try.
